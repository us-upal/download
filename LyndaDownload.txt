1. download cookies.txt in the browser extension
2. download youtube-dl.exe
3. go to working dir
4. cookies.txt to the workingDir
5. run CMD as admisistrator or in zsh:
6. youtube-dl --download-archive archive.txt --cookies cookies.txt -o "C:\Lynda\%(playlist_index)s - %(title)s.%(ext)s" https:www.YourCourseURL.com --playlist-start 1 --all-subs -f "best[height=720]"
